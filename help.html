❓ Assistance Hub

! How do I earn coins?
Practice for 60 cumulative minutes to earn 1 Store Coin. You can track your session progress on the main dashboard.
! Where can I use my discounts?
Discounts can be redeemed currently at Daraz and ShopY. Copy your unique code and apply it during checkout on their respective platforms.
! Is my progress saved?
Yes, your progress is automatically saved to your browser's local storage. Clearing your browser data will reset your stats.
